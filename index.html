Rule

If there is even one extra scoreline where the back bet wins but the lay does not lose, it is not a clean equivalent lay.

Formula notes

Normal/equivalent lay:
lay = back stake × back odds ÷ (lay odds − commission)
Free bet SNR:
lay = free bet stake × (back odds − 1) ÷ (lay odds − commission)
Refund if loses:
lay is reduced by the cash-equivalent refund value.
